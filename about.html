About Us

In 1998, Roar Davidsen established Rainy Days with a clear vision in mind. He wanted to enrich peoples lives by giving them the jackets they need so that they can push their Comfort Zone during any kind of activities.

Without having to consider the price or quality of our jackets, we have something for all possible activities. Hope you will enjoy the jackets as much as the weather.
